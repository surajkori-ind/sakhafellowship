About Sakha Fellowship

What is Sakha Fellowship?

A transformative journey designed to nurture young individuals into responsible leaders, thinkers, and changemakers — one experience at a time.

Mission

Empower youth with self-awareness, leadership skills, and community-driven purpose through experiential learning.

Vision

A world where every young person steps into their potential and creates meaningful impact.

Our Values

Learning by doing. Reflecting deeply. Growing together as a community of changemakers.

Community

Building bonds that go beyond fellowship — a lifelong network of passionate leaders.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?

What is Sakha Fellowship?

Sakha Fellowship is a transformational youth program for individuals aged 17+, including 12th passouts and college students. It focuses on self-awareness, leadership, community impact, and experiential learning through workshops, projects, mentorship, and a residential camp.

Who is eligible to apply?

Anyone aged 17+ including 12th grade passouts and college students of any stream who has a passion for learning, leadership, and community impact is eligible to apply.

Is there a fee to join the fellowship?

Please contact us directly for information about program fees. We are committed to making the fellowship accessible for all deserving young changemakers.

What is the duration of the fellowship?

The fellowship runs across several months with regular sessions, meetups, and projects, culminating in a residential camp. Exact dates will be shared after selection.

What happens after the fellowship ends?

Fellows become part of the larger Sakha community — a lifelong network of changemakers with access to ongoing mentorship, internship referrals, and future program opportunities.

How do I apply for Sakha Fellowship 2026?

Click the "Apply Now" button to fill out the Google Form. It takes about 10 minutes. Shortlisted candidates will be called for a personal interview before final selection.
