Impact · Unsung Heroes

The People Behind the Change

Every community has quiet changemakers who rarely get recognition. This series exists to find them, thank them, and tell their story.

Why we do this — Sakha fellows go looking for the people whose work keeps a community running but rarely gets noticed — teachers, caretakers, organizers, volunteers. Recognizing them is our way of saying their effort matters, and of reminding fellows that leadership often looks quiet.

Hero 01
Community Educator

Mr. Prashant Vaidya (sir)

Gujarat, India

For years, Prashant sir has worked quietly behind the scenes — mentoring young people, organizing sessions, and showing up for the community without ever expecting recognition. Fellows nominated him for the steady, unseen effort he's put in year after year.
